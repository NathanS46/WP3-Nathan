History of Before Treaty One

Before The 11 treaties the hudsons bay company owned all of the land from 1670-1870. They got this land from finding it and claiming it even though the indigious peoples of canada where already living there. The hudsons bay company tried to ignore the indigious peoples but in 1870 when the hudsons bay company sold the land to the government of canada the indiginous peoples where still on the land and didnt want to be ignored so when the government of canada took over they didnt want to be confined anymore and started to make treatys with the government of canada.
